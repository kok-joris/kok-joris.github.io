Hi there, I'm Joris Kok!

I'm a PhD candidate at the International Institute of Social History and the Institute for History, Leiden University. My dissertation project studies the social mobility of Jewish diamond workers, an important ethno-religious niche in pre-war Amsterdam. I am also involved in a project in which we have constructed a Historical Gender Gap Index for each of Sweden's counties between 1870 and 1990 in order to study the relationship between gender equality and economic growth over time and space.
